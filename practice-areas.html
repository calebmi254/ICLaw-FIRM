Constitutional Law

Constitutional Law is the body of rules and principles that govern the structure, organization, and operation of a government. It ensures the protection of individual rights, the rule of law, and the separation of powers. Constitutional Law is the foundation upon which all other laws are built, providing a framework for governance and social order.
